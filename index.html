Tennis Fixture

Hi everyone. This is an open source website to host the fixtures we would be doing.

If you want to join the fixtures, please contact me and we can add you into the tournament

The fixtures are a round robin style & are scored by wins, and losses. A win will be awarded 2 points, and loss as 1 point. The ranks will be awarded from highest score as being number 1 ranked player.

  • Win = 2 points
  • Loss = 1 point

When you complete a game, send me the results and I will update the page.
